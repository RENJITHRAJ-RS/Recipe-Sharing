Chicken Curry

⏱ Cooking Time: 30 mins | ⭐ Difficulty: Medium | 👁 Views: 45

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • Salt to taste

Cooking Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan.
  2. Add onion and garlic, sauté until golden.
  3. Add chicken and cook until browned.
  4. Add spices and simmer for 20 minutes.
